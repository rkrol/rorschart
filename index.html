Rorschart

Rorchart interprates Rails data structures for you to generate beautiful Javascript Google Charts.

Designed to be the perfect companion to rails-snowplow.

Credits

Rorchart is inpired by chartkick from Andrew Kane. Rorchart was design with a different approach of data handling and especially regarding multiple series management to allow multiple series Graphs and Tables creation right from an ActiveRecord query.
